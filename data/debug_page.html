    DPR & DCPS Athletic Reservations Requests

    Athletic Permit Seasons:
    Fall: Labor day through third Sunday in November
    Winter: Third Monday in November through third Sunday in March
    Spring: Third Monday in March through third Sunday in June
    Summer: Third Monday in June through Labor Day

    Permit Application Windows are posted at DPR.DC.GOV click HERE
    If the Permit Window is missed, the system will reopen for requests closer to the season start date.

    Indoor Facility/Rec Center Reservations Requests

    Permits inside DPR Recreation and Aquatic Centers are available at select pools, gymnasiums and multipurpose rooms by request. Search below for available locations and please read the availability description above each facility.

    Park Site Reservations for Outdoor Events

    DPR Park Sites and open spaces are currently available for rental for private events. Please search below to find available spaces, rules, and capacity limits for each site.

    Please Note:
    This process will "purchase" or "request" space depending on the facility you choose. Always check the "Status" on your receipt. All accounts created online are marked as NON-RESIDENT (and are charged the Non-resident prices) until the necessary age and residency verification documents are provided. Transactions made in a NON-RESIDENT Status will not be adjusted to Resident rates after account has been approved.


    For assistance with DPR Facility please contact the DPR Permit Office at dprpermits@dc.gov or call Customer Care @ 202-673-7647

    Rose Park T Court 1

    With over 150 Tennis Courts throughout the District it is easy to find a place to play in DC!

    Tennis courts can be permitted to individuals or organizations. Groups of 10 or more, Private instruction, Group lessons must have a permit to play on the courts.

    DPR makes some of the tennis courts at each site available for rental so you will only see half of our court inventory below. This is to ensure that there are always courts open to the public on a first come first serve basis. For courts not listed or for those listed that do not have permits we ask residents to be respectful of each other and limit play to one hour per session. If you would like to guarantee court availability or play for longer than one hour, a permit should be purchased below.

    Please note: Paid instruction, camps, or any organization/group of any size that charges for participation MUST obtain a permit to play on public courts. A fee-based permit fee is required in addition to the court fees.

    "Purchase" rooms and spaces are immediately reserve-able -- select the site, date and time; pay for your reservation and you will receive a receipt that you can present on the day of your event. Examples of spaces available for "Purchase": Most Multipurpose Rooms (not during camp dates), Park Sites, In-Season Fields, Courts (Basketball and Tennis) and Pool Lanes

    "Request" rooms and spaces will need to be assessed by a Permit Officer before rental can be approved. THESE ROOMS ARE NOT CONFIRMED UNTIL A PERMIT OFFICER CONTACTS THE CUSTOMER. A request space may look available but the Permit office will need to assess the appropriateness of the request for that particular space. DPR generally will not allow multiple private events at a single Rec center at one time. DPR recommends requesting these spaces 30 days in advance and 14 days in advance is required to process these reservations. These requests will show a zero balance on your receipt and you will be contacted by a Permit Officer within 3 business days, costs will only be assessed if and when the space is confirmed. Examples of space that are "Requested": Gymnasiums, Boxing Rings, Classrooms, Dance Rooms, Pools (use of full pool indoor and outdoor) and all Equipment (PA System, Microphone, Podium, Platform Riser, Showmobile)
    If you are requesting space during an advertised Athletic Permit window, all spaces will be "Requests", please allow 21 business days for processing.
